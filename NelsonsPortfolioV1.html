Hi, I'm Nelson Ray, a UI/UX designer a graduate from Kennesaw State University.
During my time as an undergrad, I have gained many skills.

However, along the way I realized I have a even greater passion for user interaction in particular, and so I pivoted to a degree in Interactive Design. Now I'm obsessed with learning about and creating user-centered designs.

Great design and prototypes come from great user research.
